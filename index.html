Chapter 03

Her First Year

Month by Month · Every Milestone

Twelve months of milestones, wonder and pure joy. Every smile, every first — each one a blessing we will cherish forever.

🌸
01 Month 1 Newborn

Welcome to the World, Mahika 🌸

Tiny hands, soft skin, and a cry that became the most beautiful sound in our world. One month of Mahika — and we already can't imagine life without her.

02 Month 2 With Papa

Papa's Little Girl 😊

Two months old and already Papa's best friend. She lights up when he holds her, and he lights up right back. The bond is everything.

03 Month 3 First Big Smile

That First Big Smile 😁

Three months and she gave us that first big, real smile. Not just a reflex — a proper, eyes-crinkling, heart-melting smile meant just for us.

04 Month 4 First Giggles

First Giggles 🎶

Four months old and full of personality. Giggles, curious hands reaching for everything, and expressions that make the whole family laugh.

05 Month 5 Anna Prasanna

Her First Taste of Food 🍚

Anna Prasanna — the ceremony of her very first solid food. The expression on her face when she tasted rice for the first time was absolutely priceless.

06 Month 6 Half Birthday

Happy Half Birthday, Mahika! ✨

Half a year of Mahika! We celebrated her six-month birthday and watched her try so hard to roll over — pure determination in a tiny body.

07 Month 7 Little Explorer

Hide & Seek, Fruits & Standing Up! 🌟

Seven months and Mahika's personality exploded. She loves playing hide and seek, tasted fruits for the first time, and started trying to stand up. Every day is a new adventure with this little one.

08 Month 8 Loves Going Out

Little Explorer Goes Outside 🌿

Eight months and Mahika loves going out — every outing is an adventure. She watches the world with the widest eyes, taking it all in.

09 Month 9 Playground Days

First Time at the Playground! 🎠

Nine months old and Mahika discovered the playground! Swings, slides and sand — pure joy on her face. She's getting braver and more curious every single day.

10 Month 10 Water Baby

First Swimming Pool Experience! 🏊‍♀️

Ten months old and Mahika experienced a swimming pool for the very first time! The splash, the surprise, the giggles — she took to water like a little fish.

11 Month 11 Bike Lover

She Loves Her Bike! 🚲

Eleven months and Mahika has found her favourite thing — her little bike! She lights up every time she sees it. Almost one year old, and she's already got her own set of wheels.

12 Month 12 First Birthday 🎂

First Trip to Goa & Swimming Lessons! 🏖️

One year of Mahika! We celebrated by taking her on her first trip to Goa — sand between tiny toes, ocean breeze in her hair, and her very first swimming lesson. From the hospital to the beach — what an incredible journey this year has been.
